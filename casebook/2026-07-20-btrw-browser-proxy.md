## Summary
A Chrome toolbar icon for containing one browser profile inside one proxy route. The negative-space gate beat a generic open-B lettermark and an ambiguous single-lane rail; a two-pixel amber egress packet made the final silhouette/action ownable at 16 px.

## What failed first
The bake-off's gate-cut winner stayed legible, but its black visual silhouette
collapsed into a generic `C`, so distinctiveness was only 3/5. The 148-unit
opening also left no deliberate pixel budget for an action cue. Widening the
channel to 256 units and placing one 128-unit amber packet inside it, with
64-unit top/bottom clearances, preserved a two-pixel packet plus one-pixel gaps
at 16 px. That lifted distinctiveness to 4/5 while improving balance,
scalability, and craft.
