## Summary
The multilingual local desktop bot needed one mark that could identify Snowy,
suggest translation, and remain useful as a 16px tray icon. Three lenses reached
the bake-off: a square-to-round relay, a stepped phase change, and a custom
S-ribbon. The S-ribbon won because its brand initial remained immediately legible
in monochrome while the enlarged central dovetail cut supplied the second reading:
two expressions handing the same meaning across a gap.

## What failed first
The relay finalist blacked out as a video/play/dumbbell shape, while the phase
step lost its detached diamond at 16px. The first S-ribbon review remained too
close to an ordinary letter and placed 22% of detected edge detail outside the
maskable safe circle. Enlarging the negative-space dovetail, strengthening the
asymmetric terminals, and scaling the whole mark to 88% raised distinctiveness
from 3 to 4, balance from 4 to 5, and cleared the automated maskable warning.
