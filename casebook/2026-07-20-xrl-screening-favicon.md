## Summary
Favicon for the X Reciprocity Lab Claude-style screening webpage. Essence:
**reciprocity** (curated two-way follow selection). Winning mark is a bold
continuous terracotta S-ribbon on a cream squircle — one dual-flow stroke that
reads as mutual exchange without handshake / bird / ∞ clichés. Won the bake-off
on silhouette distinctiveness and 16px clarity vs open-ring and triage-gate.

## What failed first
Pass 1 S was optically elegant at 128px but **too light at 16px** (legibility 3,
scalability 3): stroke mass and envelope left weak curve counters in the pixel
zoom. Open-ring risked loading-spinner collision; gate looked like magnet/n.
Fix: enlarge the S envelope and raise stroke ~128→148 so 16/32px still show the
dual-flow counters.
